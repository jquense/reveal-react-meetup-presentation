React-Widgets

A suite of typed or data "bound" form input components for React. For all your form and CRUD needs

With a goal towards painless mapping of Complex Values, Arrays, Numbers and Dates to user friendly inputs

heavily influenced by jquery ui and kendo ui

  <DropdownList 
    data={list} 
    defaultValue={this.state.value}/>

  <SelectList 
    data={list} 
    defaultValue={this.state.value}/>

  <Combobox 
    data={list} 
    defaultValue={this.state.value}/>

Multi value Value Pickers


  <SelectList 
    multiple
    data={list} 
    defaultValue={this.state.value}/>

  <Multiselect 
    data={list} 
    defaultValue={this.state.value}/>

Typed & Localized Inputs


  <NumberPicker 
    format='C0' 
    defaultValue={this.state.value}/>

  <DateTimePicker 
    format='MMM dd, yyyy HH:ss tt'
    defaultValue={this.state.value}/>
